# Changelog

## 2026-06-26

- Public launch of the open [Chinese Tea Dataset](https://chinatea.house/dataset/) with JSON and CSV downloads.
- Added [Chinese Tea Caffeine Chart](https://chinatea.house/chinese-tea-caffeine-chart/) and [Categories Chart](https://chinatea.house/chinese-tea-categories/) shareable visuals.
- Released dataset, caffeine chart, and categories chart on [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/ArtisanStrategies/chinatea.house/releases).
- Published companion documentation site at https://artisanstrategies.github.io/chinatea.house/.
- Integrated IndexNow submissions to notify search engines after each deploy.

## 2026-06-25

- Expanded site to 136 teas, 39 regions, and 4,477 comparisons.
- Added tea finder, brewing calculator, and occasion recommendation pages.
- Improved internal linking and added FAQPage schema.
- Added RSS feed at https://chinatea.house/feed.xml.

## Earlier

- Initial static site generator, database, and templates.
- First brewing guides and category pages.
