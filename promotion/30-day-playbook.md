# 30-Day Off-Site Promotion Playbook for chinatea.house

Goal: Earn initial backlinks, social signals, and referral traffic to help Google index and rank inner pages.

## Week 1: Foundations & Quick Wins

**Day 1 — Set up monitoring**
- Confirm Google Search Console is verified for `sc-domain:chinatea.house`
- Set up Bing Webmaster Tools if not done
- Create a simple spreadsheet to track outreach/submissions

**Day 2 — Social profiles**
- Create or update bio/links on any profiles you already have (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Reddit, Pinterest)
- Add https://chinatea.house/ to bio/link sections

**Day 3 — Reddit value post**
- Post the Reddit draft from `social-snippets.md` to r/tea
- Respond to comments for 2–3 hours after posting
- Cross-post to r/China or r/puer if relevant

**Day 4 — Pinterest**
- Pin the caffeine chart image (`/assets/chinese-tea-caffeine-chart.png`) with a link to the chart page
- Pin the categories chart
- Use descriptions from `social-snippets.md`

**Day 5 — Directory submissions**
- Submit to free directories in `directory-list.md`
- Sign up for HARO and set alerts for "tea," "coffee," "beverages," "Chinese culture"

**Day 6 — Engage in tea communities**
- Join 2–3 tea Facebook groups or forums
- Answer questions naturally; link to the site only when genuinely helpful

**Day 7 — First outreach batch**
- Send 5 personalized emails from `personalized-outreach.md`
- Targets: Teaformeplease, Teahead, Tching, World of Tea, Serious Eats

## Week 2: Content & Relationships

**Day 8 — LinkedIn post**
- Use the LinkedIn snippet from `social-snippets.md`
- Tag relevant tea professionals if appropriate

**Day 9 — Twitter/X thread**
- Post a thread: "8 Chinese teas everyone should know"
- Include the categories chart image

**Day 10 — HARO response #1**
- Respond to one journalist query using `haro-pitches.md`

**Day 11 — Second outreach batch**
- Send 5 more emails
- Targets: China Project, China Highlights, Kitchn, Tea Epicure, Food Bloggers of Canada

**Day 12 — r/dataisbeautiful**
- Post the caffeine chart dataset
- Link to `/dataset/` and `/chinese-tea-caffeine-chart/`

**Day 13 — Quora/Stack Exchange**
- Answer 3 questions about Chinese tea, caffeine, or brewing
- Link to relevant guides

**Day 14 — Follow up**
- Follow up on outreach emails sent in Week 1 (gentle bump)

## Week 3: Guest Content & Partnerships

**Day 15 — Guest post draft #1**
- Write "5 Chinese Oolongs Every Beginner Should Know"
- Pitch to 3 tea blogs

**Day 16 — Guest post draft #2**
- Write "How to Brew Gongfu Tea at Home"
- Pitch to 3 lifestyle/food blogs

**Day 17 — Podcast outreach**
- Pitch the site or yourself as a guest to 3 tea/culture podcasts

**Day 18 — YouTube/TikTok creators**
- Find 5 tea YouTubers; share the dataset or chart as a resource

**Day 19 — Third outreach batch**
- Send 5 more emails
- Targets: FlowingData, Information is Beautiful, Bon Appetit (long shot), local food bloggers

**Day 20 — HARO response #2**
- Respond to another query

## Week 4: Measurement & Iteration

**Day 21 — Check GSC and analytics**
- Look for new impressions, indexed pages, or referral traffic
- Note which pages are appearing

**Day 22 — Double down on what worked**
- If Reddit drove traffic, post another angle
- If a specific outreach got a response, send more like it

**Day 23 — Product Hunt / Hacker News**
- If you have accounts, post to Product Hunt or Hacker News Show HN
- Use the dataset or tea finder as the hook

**Day 24 — Influencer seeding**
- Reach out to 5 micro-influencers in tea/wellness
- Offer the dataset or a custom chart

**Day 25 — Fourth outreach batch**
- Send final 5 emails

**Day 26 — Update content**
- Add any new links earned to a "As Seen On" or press page

**Day 27 — Social reshare**
- Reshare top-performing post with updated copy

**Day 28 — HARO response #3**
- Final HARO response of the month

**Day 29 — Compile results**
- Count emails sent, responses, links earned, social engagements, referral traffic

**Day 30 — Plan next month**
- Based on results, choose 2–3 channels to focus on

## Success Metrics

- **Minimum viable:** 1 backlink, 5 social shares, 3 directory submissions
- **Good:** 3–5 backlinks, 1 guest post, 10+ social engagements
- **Strong:** 5+ backlinks, 2+ guest posts, measurable referral traffic
