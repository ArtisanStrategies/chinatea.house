A practical reference for brewing Chinese tea at home.

## Gongfu vs. Western style

- **Gongfu style**: high leaf-to-water ratio, short multiple infusions
- **Western style**: lower leaf-to-water ratio, one longer infusion

## Temperature and time by category

| Tea category | Temperature | Time (Western) |
|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| Green tea    | 160–180°F / 70–80°C | 2–3 min |
| White tea    | 175–185°F / 80–85°C | 3–5 min |
| Oolong tea   | 190–200°F / 88–93°C | 3–5 min |
| Black tea    | 200–212°F / 93–100°C | 3–4 min |
| Pu-erh tea   | 200–212°F / 93–100°C | 3–5 min |
