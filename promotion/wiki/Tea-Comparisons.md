# Chinese Tea Comparisons

Side-by-side comparisons of Chinese teas to help you understand the differences between similar styles.

## Live comparisons

https://chinatea.house/compare/

## Examples

- Dragon Well vs. Biluochun
- Tie Guan Yin vs. Da Hong Pao
- Silver Needle vs. White Peony
- Sheng Pu-erh vs. Shou Pu-erh

## What each comparison covers

- Origin and processing
- Flavor and aroma profile
- Caffeine level
- Best brewing approach
- Best occasion
