## Quick reference

Tea category caffeine ranges (mg per 8 oz cup):

- White tea: 15–30 mg
- Green tea: 20–45 mg
- Yellow tea: 20–40 mg
- Oolong tea: 30–50 mg
- Black tea: 40–70 mg
- Dark tea / Pu-erh: 30–60 mg
