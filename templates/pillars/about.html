About China Tea House

China Tea House is a free, independent guide to Chinese tea. We help tea drinkers explore the world of Chinese tea with clear, practical information.

Our Mission

Making Chinese Tea Accessible

Chinese tea can feel overwhelming. Hundreds of teas, dozens of regions, and countless brewing traditions make it hard to know where to start. We organize Chinese tea into clear categories, explain the basics, and let you compare teas side by side so you can find what you love.

What You'll Find

Tea Profiles, Comparisons & Guides

  • Detailed profiles for green, oolong, black, pu'er, white, yellow, dark, and scented teas.
  • Side-by-side comparisons to help you choose between teas.
  • Brewing guides for every major tea category.
  • Practical articles on caffeine, storage, gifts, tasting, and teaware.
